If you pre-register you can be allocated more shares than if you don't pre-register (if you decide to apply for shares when the Offer opens).

Pre-registration is the first stage of the Organisation Offer and provides you with an opportunity to reserve a copy of the Prospectus.

Pre-registering for a Prospectus does not commit you to buying shares - it simply lets us know that you are interested in reading the Prospectus when it becomes available.
