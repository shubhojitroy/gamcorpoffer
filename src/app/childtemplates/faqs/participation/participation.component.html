You do not need to pre-register in order to participate in the Offer.

However, if you pre-register:

you will receive a link to the electronic version of the Prospectus when it becomes available, and a link to the Prospectus and a personalised online application form when the Offer opens (or a paper copy of the Prospectus along with a personalised application form if you request it);

you will be notified of key dates during the Offer; and

if you apply for shares, you can be allocated more shares than if you don't pre-register.
