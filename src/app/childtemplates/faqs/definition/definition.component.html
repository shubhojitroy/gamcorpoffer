Organisation is the parent company of a group of wholly-owned subsidiaries (and divisions) that operate under the banner of Spring Financial Group.

Organisation's primary operating subsidiaries and divisions include Group Pty. Ltd.; Equities (division); Realty Pty Ltd; Accounting Pty Ltd; Finance Pty Ltd; and Wealthadviser Financial Education (division).

Through its operating subsidiaries and divisions Organisation provides a range of products and services that include financial planning and investment advice; insurance and superannuation advice and products; finance advice and products; tax & accounting services; and financial education publications.
